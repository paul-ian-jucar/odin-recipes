Doritos Sausage Balls

These doritos sausage balls with 2 types of sausages combine two of the all-time greatest finger foods in one. Perfect for tailgating or holiday parties.

By Nicole McLaughlin Published on September 16, 2024

Ingridients

  • 1/2 pound fresh Mexican chorizo, casings removed
  • 1/2 pound ground pork or milkd breakfast sausage, casings removed
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose baking mix (such as Bisquick®)
  • ¾ cup finely ground nacho cheese-flavored chips (such as Doritos®)
  • 1 (8-ounce) package pre-shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped pickled jalapeno
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup prepared salsa
